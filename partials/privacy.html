Privacy Policy

Why do I have to log in with Facebook?

CalPoly Textbooks is concerned with making your experience buying and selling textbooks with other students, quick, easy, and useful.

In order to facilitate communication with other students, you're required to use your Facebook account to connect to other students.

Your personal data is not used for any purpose other than connecting buyers to you through Facebook.

What information does CalPolyTextbooks use?

CalPoly Textbooks uses your Facebook display name and profile picture.

Why do I need to give you Facebook publishing permissions??

In order to put your books on CP Textbook Exchange, we need permission to post on your behalf. All posts will be presented to you the way they'll be displayed on Facebook before we create them, and you always have the option to opt-out of publication on Facebook.
